Global Warming: A Growing Crisis

Understand the impact of global warming and how we can mitigate it.

What is Global Warming?

Global warming refers to the long-term rise in Earth's average temperature due to human activities like deforestation, fossil fuel burning, and other pollution. This leads to climate change, rising sea levels, and more extreme weather events.

Causes of Global Warming

Burning of Fossil Fuels (Coal, Oil, Natural Gas)
Deforestation
Industrial Emissions
Agricultural Practices
Waste Management

Effects of Global Warming

The impact of global warming is evident in rising sea levels, melting glaciers, intense heatwaves, and more frequent storms. These changes threaten ecosystems, biodiversity, and human livelihoods.

What Can We Do?

We can fight global warming through renewable energy, energy conservation, sustainable agriculture, and reducing waste. Everyone has a role to play.
